International Shipping Documentation

When sending parcels internationally, it's essential to have the correct documents ready for the shipping process. These include:

  • Commercial Invoice

  • Battery Statement

  • Tax Documents and Shipping Labels

  • Packing List (for commercial shipments)

Some items may require customs clearance during the shipping process. Our team will guide you to ensure compliance with Austria's import rules.

Duty & Tax in Austria

All international shipments are subject to import duties and taxes as regulated by Austrian customs. When using our courier service from Singapore to Austria, we recommend being aware of these charges, as they differ depending on the shipment's value and the destination's regulations.

Austria Duty & Tax Information

  • Currency: Euro (EUR)

  • Calculation Method: CIF (Cost, Insurance, and Freight)

  • Tax Threshold: 0 EUR

  • Tax (VAT/GST): 20%

  • Duty Threshold: 150 EUR

Tax & duties are calculated on the price of the products and shipping costs.

Austria Shipping Restrictions & Prohibited Items

Shipping to Austria can be complex due to customs restrictions and regulations; however, our courier company can assist. It's important to check whether your parcel requires special arrangements or is permitted to enter the country before sending.

Austria Prohibited Items

❌Drugs and controlled substances

❌Weapons and ammunition

❌Counterfeit goods

❌Certain food products without proper certification

❌Publications that violate regulations

❌Items that may endanger public safety

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Is shipping from Singapore to Austria difficult?

Shipping parcels from Singapore to Austria is straightforward when using a trusted courier like Airpak Express. As an experienced freight forwarder, you can enjoy efficient shipping and secure delivery as we ensure your shipments from Singapore to Austria reach their destination without hassle.

2. How much time does it take to deliver a parcel to Austria?

Delivery times vary depending on the type of shipment and the courier chosen. Typically, shipping from Singapore to Austria takes between 5 to 10 business days for standard international shipping. Express options are available for faster delivery.

3. What is the cost to deliver to Austria?

Shipping cost depends on factors such as parcel weight, dimensions, and the shipping rate of your chosen courier service. To get an accurate shipping cost for parcels from Singapore to Austria, contact us for a tailored quote based on your specific shipment.

4. How should I pack my item(s) for international shipping?

Proper packing is essential to protect your parcel during transit. Use sturdy boxes, cushioning materials, and secure seals. Label your parcel clearly, and if shipping fragile items, mark them accordingly to ensure safe handling throughout international shipping from Singapore to Austria.

5. How can I track my shipment to Austria?

Every Airpak Express shipment comes with end-to-end tracking. You'll always know where your parcel is, and in case of customs delays, our team will notify you and assist with any required follow-up.

6. Do I need insurance to ship my products to Austria?

While insurance is optional, it is highly recommended for valuable shipments. Insurance offers peace of mind and financial protection in case of loss or damage during international shipping from Singapore to Austria.

7. What should I do if my parcel is lost during transit?

If your parcel is lost, contact your courier service immediately with your tracking details. We will assist you in tracing the shipment and initiating any claims needed to resolve the issue promptly and ensure your delivery is secure.

8. Are there any prohibited items when shipping to Autria?

Yes, Austria has a list of prohibited items that cannot be imported. These include drugs, weapons, counterfeit goods, and certain food products without proper certification. Always check the latest regulations before sending a parcel from Singapore to Austria to avoid shipment delays or confiscation.

9. What types of shipment options are available for Singapore to Austria?

You can choose from air freight, sea freight, or express courier services depending on your budget and urgency. Each type offers different shipping rates and delivery times to suit your needs through our international courier service.
